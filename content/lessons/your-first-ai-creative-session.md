## narration
Forget everything you know about prompting. Creative AI work is not about precise instructions and structured frameworks. It is a conversation. A jam session. A brainstorm with the most well-read collaborator you have ever had. In this lesson, you will learn how to run your first AI creative session.
highlight: conversation, jam session, brainstorm, collaborator

## concept: Creative Session Flow
- Set the Mood (0.15, 0.5)
- Open Wide (0.35, 0.5)
- Explore Branches (0.55, 0.5)
- Curate (0.75, 0.5)
- Refine (0.9, 0.5)

## narration
Step one is setting the mood. Before you ask AI to generate anything, give it creative context. Not a corporate brief — a vibe. Tell it the emotion you want. The aesthetic. The references. Say things like "think Wes Anderson meets cyberpunk" or "the feeling of a rainy Sunday with a good book." AI responds to creative direction just like a human collaborator does.
highlight: setting the mood, vibe, emotion, aesthetic, references

## narration
Step two is opening wide. Ask for volume. Say "give me twenty concepts" not "give me a concept." Say "what are the wildest possible angles on this?" not "write me a headline." The first five ideas from AI will be obvious. Ideas six through fifteen get interesting. Ideas sixteen through twenty get weird — and weird is where the gold lives.
highlight: twenty concepts, wildest possible angles, weird, gold lives

## narration
Step three is exploring branches. When one idea sparks something, follow it. Say "I love number fourteen — give me ten variations on that theme." Then pick the best variation and go deeper. This is how creative sessions work — you are not looking for the answer on the first try. You are navigating a tree of possibilities, pruning as you go.
highlight: exploring branches, ten variations, tree of possibilities, pruning

## code: Creative Prompt Example
```
I'm working on a brand identity for a
meditation app for people who hate
meditation. Think rebellious wellness.

Give me 15 tagline concepts. Mix tones:
some irreverent, some poetic, some
brutally honest. Surprise me.
```
highlight-lines: 1,2,3,5,7
duration: 7

## narration
Step four is curation. This is your superpower. AI gave you volume — now you bring taste. Pick the three ideas that resonate. The ones that feel fresh, not formulaic. The ones your audience would actually connect with. This step is entirely human. No AI can do it for you because no AI has your taste.
highlight: curation, superpower, taste, fresh, entirely human

## narration
Step five is refinement. Take your curated favorites back to AI. Say "refine this — make it sharper, more specific, with this particular tone." Iterate three or four rounds. Each round gets closer to something you are proud of. The final output is a collaboration — your vision, amplified by AI's speed.
highlight: refinement, iterate, collaboration, your vision, AI's speed

## timeline
1. Mood | Share the vibe, references, emotion — 2 minutes
2. Generate | Ask for 15-20 concepts — 1 minute
3. Branch | Follow the best ideas deeper — 5 minutes
4. Curate | Pick the top 3 with your taste — 2 minutes
5. Refine | Iterate 3-4 rounds to polish — 5 minutes
duration: 7

## narration
That is your first creative session. Fifteen minutes. Dozens of concepts explored. Three polished ideas you can actually use. Try this today with any creative brief you have — a social post, a brand name, a story concept, a design direction. The blank page is dead. You never have to start from zero again.
highlight: fifteen minutes, dozens of concepts, blank page is dead, never start from zero

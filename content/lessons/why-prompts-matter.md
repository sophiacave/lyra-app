## narration
Here is what most people get wrong. They type a one-sentence question and expect a masterpiece. Then they say AI is overhyped. But AI is a mirror. It reflects the clarity of your thinking back at you. A vague question gets a vague answer. A precise, thoughtful prompt gets output that feels like it read your mind.
highlight: mirror, clarity, precise, thoughtful

## narration
Think of AI as the world's most capable intern. It has read virtually everything ever published. It can write in any style, format, or language. It works instantly and never gets tired. But it has zero knowledge of your specific situation. Every prompt you write is a briefing document. The better the briefing, the better the work.
highlight: intern, briefing document, specific situation

<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The System</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">From Tools to Engine</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Individual AI skills are useful. A connected AI system is transformational. Over the past 9 lessons, you've learned how to use AI for research, outreach, calls, proposals, objections, pipeline management, retention, and analytics. Now it's time to wire them together into a machine that runs every day.</p>
  <p class="section-text">The goal: a daily rhythm where AI handles 80% of the preparation, analysis, and drafting — and you focus 100% of your energy on the human moments that close deals.</p>
</div>

<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Daily Rhythm</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">The AI-Powered Sales Day</h2>
  <div class="demo-container">
    <p><strong style="color: var(--blue);">Morning Block (30 min):</strong> AI pipeline review + prospect research for today's calls</p>
    <p><strong style="color: var(--green);">Mid-Morning:</strong> Calls and meetings (you're fully prepped, AI did the homework)</p>
    <p><strong style="color: var(--purple);">Lunch:</strong> AI generates follow-ups from morning calls + new outreach batch</p>
    <p><strong style="color: var(--orange);">Afternoon:</strong> Proposals, presentations, strategic work (AI drafts, you refine)</p>
    <p><strong style="color: var(--red);">End of Day (15 min):</strong> AI reviews today's activity, plans tomorrow's priorities</p>
  </div>
  <p class="section-text">That's it. 45 minutes of AI work bookending a day of high-value human selling. The AI handles the grunt work. You handle the relationships.</p>
</div>

<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Toolkit</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Your Saved Prompt Library</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Build a personal prompt library organized by sales stage. Save the prompts that work best for your industry, your product, and your style. Refine them weekly based on results.</p>
  <div class="demo-container">
    <p><strong>Research:</strong> Prospect brief prompt (Lesson 2)</p>
    <p><strong>Outreach:</strong> PRISM email + sequence builder (Lesson 3)</p>
    <p><strong>Call Prep:</strong> 5-minute call brief (Lesson 4)</p>
    <p><strong>Proposals:</strong> Tailored proposal generator (Lesson 5)</p>
    <p><strong>Objections:</strong> HEARD method playbook (Lesson 6)</p>
    <p><strong>Pipeline:</strong> Weekly audit + forecast (Lesson 7)</p>
    <p><strong>Retention:</strong> Health check + upsell finder (Lesson 8)</p>
    <p><strong>Analytics:</strong> Performance review + win/loss analysis (Lesson 9)</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="try-it-box">
  <h3>Build Your System Now</h3>
  <p>Create your personalized AI sales playbook:</p>
  <div class="prompt-box"><code>I sell [YOUR PRODUCT/SERVICE] to [TARGET MARKET] at [PRICE POINT]. My sales cycle is typically [LENGTH]. Create a complete daily AI sales system for me with: 1) Morning routine prompts (pipeline review + research), 2) Pre-call prep template, 3) Post-call follow-up template, 4) Weekly analytics review prompt, 5) Monthly strategic planning prompt. Customize every prompt for my specific product and market. This is my personal sales operating system.</code></div>
</div>

<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Scaling</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Growing Your AI Sales Engine</h2>
  <p class="section-text">As your business grows, your AI system scales with you. Feed it more data and it gives better insights. Build more templates and your team gets consistent. Connect AI workflows to your CRM and automation tools and the system starts running itself.</p>
  <p class="section-text">The salespeople who build these systems now will dominate their markets for years. Not because AI replaces human selling — but because it frees human sellers to do what they do best: connect, understand, and solve real problems for real people.</p>
</div>

<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Finish Line</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">You're Ready</h2>
  <p class="section-text">You've completed AI for Sales & Revenue. You have the frameworks, the prompts, and the system. The only thing left is to use it. Start tomorrow morning with the daily rhythm. Refine as you go. Let the results speak for themselves.</p>
  <p class="section-text">Sales has always been about helping people solve problems. AI just lets you help more people, faster, with more insight. That's not replacing the art of selling — that's perfecting it.</p>
</div>

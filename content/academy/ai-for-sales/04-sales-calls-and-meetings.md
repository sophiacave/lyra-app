<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Pre-Call</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">The 5-Minute Call Prep</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Walking into a sales call unprepared is like showing up to a job interview in pajamas. AI eliminates every excuse. In five minutes, you can have a complete call brief: prospect background, likely objections, tailored questions, and a call flow that guides the conversation toward a close.</p>
  <p class="section-text">This isn't about scripting every word. It's about having a map so you never get lost in the conversation. The best salespeople prepare obsessively and then sound completely natural. AI makes the preparation effortless.</p>
</div>

<div class="try-it-box">
  <h3>Try It Now</h3>
  <p>Prep for your next sales call in one prompt:</p>
  <div class="prompt-box"><code>I have a sales call in 1 hour with [NAME, TITLE] at [COMPANY]. I sell [PRODUCT/SERVICE]. This is a [first call / follow-up / demo / closing call]. Give me: 1) A 30-second opening that builds rapport, 2) Five discovery questions tailored to their role, 3) Three ways to position my product for their specific situation, 4) Likely objections and one-line responses, 5) A natural close or next-step ask.</code></div>
</div>

<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">During the Call</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">AI-Informed Conversation Guides</h2>
  <p class="section-text">You can't read from a script on a live call. But you can have a one-page cheat sheet with key questions, pivot points, and value statements. AI generates these beautifully.</p>
  <div class="demo-container">
    <p><strong style="color: var(--green);">Discovery Phase:</strong> "What's the biggest bottleneck in your [process] right now?"</p>
    <p><strong style="color: var(--blue);">Pain Amplification:</strong> "What happens if that problem isn't solved in the next 6 months?"</p>
    <p><strong style="color: var(--purple);">Solution Bridge:</strong> "What if you could [specific outcome] without [their current pain]?"</p>
    <p><strong style="color: var(--orange);">Close Signal:</strong> "If we could show you this working with your data, would that be worth a deeper look?"</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Post-Call</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Follow-Up That Seals the Deal</h2>
  <p class="section-text">The follow-up email is where most deals die. Salespeople send generic "great to chat" messages that add zero value. AI fixes this by turning your call notes into a personalized follow-up that recaps key points, addresses concerns raised, and creates a clear path forward.</p>
  <p class="section-text">Send this within 30 minutes of the call. Speed signals professionalism and urgency.</p>
</div>

<div class="try-it-box">
  <h3>Post-Call Follow-Up</h3>
  <p>Turn your call notes into a perfect follow-up:</p>
  <div class="prompt-box"><code>I just had a sales call with [NAME] at [COMPANY]. Here are my notes: [PASTE RAW NOTES]. Write a follow-up email that: 1) Thanks them specifically for something they said, 2) Recaps the 2-3 key pain points they mentioned, 3) Maps our solution to those specific pains, 4) Addresses the concern they raised about [OBJECTION], 5) Proposes a clear next step with a specific date/time. Keep it under 200 words. Professional but warm.</code></div>
</div>

<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Pro Tip</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Build a Call Intelligence System</h2>
  <p class="section-text">After every call, feed your notes to AI and ask for patterns. Over time, you'll build a dataset of what works: which questions open people up, which objections come up most, what closes deals. This is your personal sales playbook, refined by real data.</p>
</div>

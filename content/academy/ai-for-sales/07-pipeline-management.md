<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Mess</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Your Pipeline Is Lying to You</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Most sales pipelines are fiction. Deals that should be dead are sitting in "negotiation." Prospects who ghosted three weeks ago are still marked "interested." The pipeline shows $500K in opportunity, but the real number is half that.</p>
  <p class="section-text">AI brings honesty to your pipeline. It can analyze each deal based on objective criteria — last contact date, engagement signals, deal stage duration, buyer behavior — and tell you which deals are real and which are wishful thinking.</p>
</div>

<div class="try-it-box">
  <h3>Try It Now</h3>
  <p>Run a pipeline audit:</p>
  <div class="prompt-box"><code>Here's my current sales pipeline: [PASTE DEAL LIST WITH: Company, Deal Size, Stage, Last Contact Date, Notes]. Analyze each deal and categorize as: ALIVE (active engagement, likely to close), ON LIFE SUPPORT (stalled but recoverable with the right action), or DEAD (remove from pipeline). For each deal, suggest the single best next action to move it forward or confirm it's dead.</code></div>
</div>

<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Workflow</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">The AI-Powered Weekly Review</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Every Monday morning, feed your pipeline to AI for a strategic review. This replaces the hour-long pipeline meeting with a 15-minute focused session.</p>
  <div class="demo-container">
    <p><strong style="color: var(--green);">Review 1:</strong> Which deals moved forward last week? Celebrate and accelerate.</p>
    <p><strong style="color: var(--orange);">Review 2:</strong> Which deals stalled? Diagnose why and plan re-engagement.</p>
    <p><strong style="color: var(--red);">Review 3:</strong> Which deals should be killed? Free up mental energy.</p>
    <p><strong style="color: var(--blue);">Review 4:</strong> Where are the gaps? Do you need more top-of-funnel activity?</p>
    <p><strong style="color: var(--purple);">Review 5:</strong> What's the realistic close forecast for this month?</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Forecasting</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">AI Deal Forecasting</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Traditional forecasting is guesswork dressed up in a spreadsheet. AI forecasting uses patterns: how long deals typically stay in each stage, what engagement patterns predict closes, and which deal characteristics correlate with wins.</p>
  <p class="section-text">Feed AI your won and lost deals from the past 6 months, and it can identify the patterns that predict success. Then apply those patterns to your current pipeline for a forecast grounded in data, not hope.</p>
</div>

<div class="try-it-box">
  <h3>Forecast Your Month</h3>
  <div class="prompt-box"><code>Based on this pipeline data: [PASTE DEALS WITH STAGES AND SIZES]. My average sales cycle is [X WEEKS]. My historical close rate by stage: [e.g., Demo=30%, Proposal=50%, Negotiation=75%]. Calculate a realistic revenue forecast for this month with confidence levels (best case, likely case, worst case). Flag any deals that are taking longer than average for their stage.</code></div>
</div>

<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">System</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Build Your Pipeline Rhythm</h2>
  <p class="section-text">The best pipeline managers don't manage deals — they manage a system. Monday: AI pipeline review. Wednesday: re-engagement outreach for stalled deals. Friday: new opportunity planning. AI makes each of these sessions fast and focused. The system runs itself once you build it.</p>
</div>

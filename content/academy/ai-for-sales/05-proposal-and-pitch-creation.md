<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Problem</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Proposals Are a Time Trap</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Most salespeople dread proposals. They take hours to write, they're often copy-pasted from old ones, and they rarely speak directly to the prospect's specific situation. That's why most proposals lose — they're generic documents dressed up with a new logo.</p>
  <p class="section-text">AI turns proposal creation from a half-day chore into a 30-minute sprint. More importantly, it makes every proposal genuinely customized because you can feed it everything you learned from your discovery calls.</p>
</div>

<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Framework</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">The Winning Proposal Structure</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Every great proposal follows this arc — it mirrors how buyers actually make decisions:</p>
  <div class="demo-container">
    <p><strong style="color: var(--red);">1. Their Problem:</strong> Show you understand their pain better than they do</p>
    <p><strong style="color: var(--orange);">2. Cost of Inaction:</strong> What happens if they don't solve this? Make it real.</p>
    <p><strong style="color: var(--blue);">3. Your Solution:</strong> Mapped specifically to their problems (not a feature list)</p>
    <p><strong style="color: var(--green);">4. Proof:</strong> Case studies, results, testimonials from similar companies</p>
    <p><strong style="color: var(--purple);">5. Investment & ROI:</strong> Frame price as investment. Show the math.</p>
    <p><strong style="color: var(--accent);">6. Next Steps:</strong> Make saying yes easy. Remove friction.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="try-it-box">
  <h3>Try It Now</h3>
  <p>Generate a proposal outline from your call notes:</p>
  <div class="prompt-box"><code>Create a proposal outline for [COMPANY]. They need [WHAT THEY NEED]. Their main pain points from our calls: [LIST PAINS]. Our solution: [YOUR PRODUCT/SERVICE]. Pricing: [YOUR PRICING]. Write each section with specific language tailored to their situation. Include a compelling executive summary (3 sentences max) and an ROI calculation showing the value vs. cost. Tone: confident, not pushy.</code></div>
</div>

<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Pitch Decks</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">AI-Generated Pitch Outlines</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Need a pitch deck for a presentation? AI can outline every slide with talking points. You handle the design and delivery — AI handles the structure and messaging.</p>
  <p class="section-text">The key is feeding AI your discovery notes so the pitch speaks directly to what the prospect cares about. A pitch that opens with their specific challenge and ends with their specific ROI wins every time.</p>
</div>

<div class="try-it-box">
  <h3>Build a Pitch Deck Outline</h3>
  <div class="prompt-box"><code>Create a 10-slide pitch deck outline for presenting to [COMPANY]. Their key decision-makers: [NAMES/ROLES]. Their challenges: [LIST THEM]. For each slide, give me: the headline, 3 bullet points, and talking notes. Focus on their ROI, not our features. End with a clear call to action.</code></div>
</div>

<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Speed Wins</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">The Fast-Proposal Advantage</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Deals have momentum. Every day between "send me a proposal" and actually sending it, your close rate drops. AI lets you send a polished, customized proposal the same day — often within hours of the call. That speed signals competence, eagerness, and professionalism. It also beats your slower competitors to the finish line.</p>
</div>

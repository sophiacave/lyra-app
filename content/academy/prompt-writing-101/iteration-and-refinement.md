<!-- SECTION 1 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Mindset</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Stop expecting perfection on the first try.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Here's a secret that expert AI users know: <strong>they don't write perfect prompts.</strong> They write good-enough prompts and then refine through conversation.</p>
  <p class="section-text">Think of it like sculpting. You start with a rough shape and carve it into something beautiful. Trying to get the final result in one prompt is like trying to sculpt a masterpiece with a single cut of the chisel.</p>
  <p class="section-text">The conversation IS the tool. The back-and-forth IS the process. Embrace it.</p>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 2: 4 PATTERNS -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Patterns</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">4 follow-up patterns that fix anything.</h2>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.75rem">
    <div style="display:flex;flex-direction:column;gap:20px">
      <div>
        <div style="display:flex;gap:10px;align-items:center;margin-bottom:8px">
          <div style="background:rgba(251,146,60,.12);color:var(--orange);font-weight:800;font-size:.75rem;padding:4px 10px;border-radius:6px">1</div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.95rem">The Zoom In</div>
        </div>
        <div style="color:var(--dim);font-size:.85rem;margin-bottom:6px">When the output is too broad or surface-level.</div>
        <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(251,146,60,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--text)">
          "Good start. Now expand on point #3 with specific examples and data."<br>
          "Go deeper on the pricing section. Give me exact numbers and a comparison table."
        </div>
      </div>

      <div>
        <div style="display:flex;gap:10px;align-items:center;margin-bottom:8px">
          <div style="background:rgba(192,132,252,.12);color:var(--purple);font-weight:800;font-size:.75rem;padding:4px 10px;border-radius:6px">2</div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.95rem">The Tone Shift</div>
        </div>
        <div style="color:var(--dim);font-size:.85rem;margin-bottom:6px">When the content is right but the voice is wrong.</div>
        <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(192,132,252,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--text)">
          "Rewrite this but make it sound like a friend giving advice, not a textbook."<br>
          "Same content, but more confident. Remove all hedging language."
        </div>
      </div>

      <div>
        <div style="display:flex;gap:10px;align-items:center;margin-bottom:8px">
          <div style="background:rgba(74,222,128,.12);color:var(--green);font-weight:800;font-size:.75rem;padding:4px 10px;border-radius:6px">3</div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.95rem">The Redirect</div>
        </div>
        <div style="color:var(--dim);font-size:.85rem;margin-bottom:6px">When it went in the wrong direction entirely.</div>
        <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(74,222,128,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--text)">
          "Not quite. I don't need the history — I need actionable steps I can implement today."<br>
          "You focused on enterprise. I need this for a solo freelancer with no team."
        </div>
      </div>

      <div>
        <div style="display:flex;gap:10px;align-items:center;margin-bottom:8px">
          <div style="background:rgba(56,189,248,.12);color:var(--blue);font-weight:800;font-size:.75rem;padding:4px 10px;border-radius:6px">4</div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.95rem">The Remix</div>
        </div>
        <div style="color:var(--dim);font-size:.85rem;margin-bottom:6px">When you want variations or alternatives.</div>
        <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(56,189,248,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--text)">
          "Give me 3 completely different versions of this headline."<br>
          "Keep the structure but replace all the examples with ones from healthcare."
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 3 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Decision</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">When to iterate vs when to start over.</h2>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem">
    <div style="display:grid;grid-template-columns:1fr 1fr;gap:16px">
      <div>
        <div style="font-size:.7rem;font-weight:700;color:var(--green);text-transform:uppercase;letter-spacing:1px;margin-bottom:8px">Iterate (refine what you have)</div>
        <ul style="list-style:none;padding:0;margin:0;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)">
          <li style="padding:6px 0;border-bottom:1px solid var(--border)">✓ The structure is right, details are off</li>
          <li style="padding:6px 0;border-bottom:1px solid var(--border)">✓ Tone needs adjusting</li>
          <li style="padding:6px 0;border-bottom:1px solid var(--border)">✓ Need more depth on specific sections</li>
          <li style="padding:6px 0">✓ 70%+ of the output is usable</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div>
        <div style="font-size:.7rem;font-weight:700;color:var(--red);text-transform:uppercase;letter-spacing:1px;margin-bottom:8px">Start Over (new prompt)</div>
        <ul style="list-style:none;padding:0;margin:0;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)">
          <li style="padding:6px 0;border-bottom:1px solid var(--border)">✗ Completely wrong direction</li>
          <li style="padding:6px 0;border-bottom:1px solid var(--border)">✗ Wrong format entirely</li>
          <li style="padding:6px 0;border-bottom:1px solid var(--border)">✗ 3+ rounds of iteration with no improvement</li>
          <li style="padding:6px 0">✗ You realized your original ask was wrong</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <p class="section-text" style="margin-top:1rem"><strong>Pro tip:</strong> When starting over, don't just re-send the same prompt. Analyze <em>why</em> the first attempt failed and fix that in your new prompt. Bad iteration is doing the same thing and expecting different results.</p>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 4 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Power Move</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">The "best of both" technique.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">When you have two outputs and each has parts you like:</p>
  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem;background:var(--surface);border:1px solid var(--border2)">
    <div style="font-size:.85rem;color:var(--text);line-height:1.8">"I like the opening from version 1 and the structure from version 2. Combine them: use version 1's intro paragraph, then follow version 2's format for the rest. Keep version 2's bullet points but make them punchier like version 1's writing style."</div>
  </div>
  <p class="section-text" style="margin-top:1rem">AI excels at synthesis. Tell it exactly which pieces to combine and you'll get something better than either version alone.</p>
</div>

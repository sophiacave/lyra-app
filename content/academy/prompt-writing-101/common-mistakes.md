<pre style="margin:0;color:#e5e5e5"><code><span style="color:#ef4444">BROKEN:</span>
"I need a business plan, market analysis, financial projections,
marketing strategy, competitive landscape, team structure, and
a pitch deck outline for my new app idea."

<span style="color:#71717a">DIAGNOSIS: 7 asks in one prompt. Each one deserves its own
focused conversation. The output will be shallow on everything.</span>

<span style="color:#34d399">FIXED:</span>
"I am building a meal-planning app for busy parents. Start with
a competitive landscape: identify the top 5 competitors, their
pricing, and one gap each of them has. Format as a table with
columns: App Name, Monthly Price, Target User, Key Gap."

<span style="color:#71717a">Then follow up for each subsequent section in separate prompts.</span></code></pre>

<div style="font-size:.7rem;color:#71717a;margin-bottom:.5rem;text-transform:uppercase;letter-spacing:.05em">Correction 2 — The Invisible Audience</div>
<pre style="margin:0;color:#e5e5e5"><code><span style="color:#ef4444">BROKEN:</span>
"Explain blockchain technology and its potential impact on
the financial services industry."

<span style="color:#71717a">DIAGNOSIS: No audience. This could be for a 10-year-old or a
bank CTO. The AI will default to Wikipedia-level generic.</span>

<span style="color:#34d399">FIXED:</span>
"Explain blockchain to a traditional bank's risk management
team. They understand finance but not distributed systems.
Focus on 3 specific impacts: settlement speed, fraud reduction,
and regulatory compliance. For each, give one real-world example
from a bank that has implemented it. Under 400 words."</code></pre>
</div>

<div style="font-size:.7rem;color:#71717a;margin-bottom:.5rem;text-transform:uppercase;letter-spacing:.05em">Correction 3 — The Tone-Deaf Prompt</div>
<pre style="margin:0;color:#e5e5e5"><code><span style="color:#ef4444">BROKEN:</span>
"Write a message to my team about the layoffs."

<span style="color:#71717a">DIAGNOSIS: No tone, no context about severity, no guidance on
what to emphasize. The AI could write something callous or
something so soft it buries the message.</span>

<span style="color:#34d399">FIXED:</span>
"Write a message from a VP of Engineering to a 40-person team.
We are laying off 6 people next week (they have been notified
separately). Tone: honest, empathetic, and direct — do not
sugarcoat but do not be cold. Address: why this happened
(revenue miss), who is affected (roles, not names), what
support is available (severance, job placement), and what
the remaining team should expect. Under 300 words."</code></pre>

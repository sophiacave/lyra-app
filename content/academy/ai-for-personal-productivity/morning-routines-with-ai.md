<!-- SECTION 1 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Why Mornings Matter</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">The first hour sets the tone. AI helps you own it.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Most productivity advice gives you a cookie-cutter morning routine. Wake up at 5am. Meditate. Journal. Cold shower. But that doesn't work if you have kids, chronic pain, a night shift, or you're just not a morning person.</p>
  <p class="section-text">AI doesn't judge your schedule. It works with what you have. Tell it your real constraints — when you wake up, what your mornings actually look like, what you wish was different — and it builds a routine around your reality.</p>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 2 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Technique</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">The context dump: give AI your real morning.</h2>
  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.75rem">
    <p style="margin-bottom:1rem">The more honest context you provide, the better the plan. Here's what to include:</p>
    <div style="background:var(--bg);padding:1rem;border-radius:8px;margin-bottom:1rem">
      <p style="color:var(--orange);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.5rem">Include in your context dump:</p>
      <ul style="font-size:0.95rem;line-height:1.7">
        <li>What time you actually wake up (not what you wish)</li>
        <li>Non-negotiables (kids' school bus, medication, pet care)</li>
        <li>What drains you in the morning (decision fatigue, rushing)</li>
        <li>What you wish you had time for (exercise, quiet coffee, reading)</li>
        <li>Your biggest morning frustration</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    <p style="color:var(--dim);font-size:0.9rem">This isn't oversharing — it's giving your assistant the information it needs to actually help. A vague ask gets a generic plan. A real ask gets a real plan.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 3 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">In Practice</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">A real morning plan, built in one prompt.</h2>
  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem">
    <div style="background:var(--bg);padding:1rem;border-radius:8px;margin-bottom:1rem">
      <p style="color:var(--purple);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.5rem">Example prompt:</p>
      <code style="font-size:0.9rem;line-height:1.6">"I wake up at 7:15am and need to leave for work by 8:30. I have a dog that needs a quick walk. I always skip breakfast and regret it by 10am. I'd love 10 minutes of quiet before the chaos starts. Can you build me a realistic morning schedule?"</code>
    </div>
    <div style="background:var(--bg);padding:1rem;border-radius:8px">
      <p style="color:var(--green);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.5rem">What AI gives you:</p>
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem;line-height:1.6">A minute-by-minute schedule that accounts for the dog walk, includes a 5-minute breakfast prep (overnight oats suggestion), and carves out those 10 quiet minutes — all within your real timeline. No 5am wake-up required.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 4 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Level Up</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">When the plan breaks — ask AI to fix it.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Plans break. You oversleep. The dog gets into something. A kid has a meltdown. The power of AI is that you can say <em>"Hey, I woke up 20 minutes late — adjust my morning plan"</em> and get an updated version instantly.</p>
  <p class="section-text">This is the shift: your plan isn't carved in stone. It's a living thing you can reshape in seconds. That flexibility is what makes AI planning actually sustainable.</p>
</div>

<!-- TRY IT -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Try It</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Build your personal morning routine.</h2>
  <div class="try-it-box">
    <p>Do a context dump about your real morning. Be honest — the messy truth works better than the ideal version. Use this template:</p>
    <div class="prompt-box">
      <code>Here's my real morning situation: I wake up at [TIME]. I need to leave by [TIME]. My non-negotiables are [LIST THEM]. I always struggle with [PROBLEM]. I wish I had time for [WISH]. Build me a realistic morning routine that actually works for my life.</code>
    </div>
    <p style="margin-top:1rem;color:var(--dim)">After you get the plan, try a follow-up: "What if I oversleep by 15 minutes?" See how fast AI adapts the schedule.</p>
  </div>
</div>

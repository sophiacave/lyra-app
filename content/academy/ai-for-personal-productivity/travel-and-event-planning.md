<!-- SECTION 1 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Why This Works</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Planning is just organized decision-making. AI is built for this.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Planning a trip or event involves hundreds of small decisions: where to stay, what to do, when to do it, how to get there, what to bring. Each decision is simple on its own, but together they're overwhelming.</p>
  <p class="section-text">AI doesn't get overwhelmed. It takes your constraints (budget, dates, preferences, group size) and builds a complete plan. You review, adjust, and go. The hours you'd spend on travel blogs and Pinterest boards become a 10-minute conversation.</p>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 2 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Travel Planning</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Constraints first, then let AI build the dream.</h2>
  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.75rem">
    <p style="margin-bottom:1rem">The more constraints you give, the more realistic and useful the plan. Include:</p>
    <div style="background:var(--bg);padding:1rem;border-radius:8px;margin-bottom:1rem">
      <p style="color:var(--orange);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.5rem">Example travel prompt:</p>
      <code style="font-size:0.9rem;line-height:1.6">"Plan a 4-day trip to Nashville for 2 people. Budget: $1,500 total including hotel. We love live music, good food, and walkable neighborhoods. We don't want a packed schedule — leave room to wander. We're driving from Atlanta. Give me a day-by-day itinerary with restaurant suggestions and one backup plan for rain."</code>
    </div>
    <p style="font-size:0.9rem;color:var(--dim)">AI will build a realistic itinerary with morning, afternoon, and evening activities, restaurant picks near each activity, drive time estimates, and that rain backup. Then you can say "swap Day 2 lunch for something cheaper" or "add a brewery tour" and it adjusts.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 3 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Event Planning</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Birthdays, dinners, reunions — AI is your event coordinator.</h2>
  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem">
    <div style="margin-bottom:1.25rem">
      <p style="color:var(--purple);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">Birthday party</p>
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem">"I'm planning my daughter's 7th birthday party. 12 kids. Budget: $200. We have a backyard. Give me a theme, activities for 2 hours, a simple food menu, and a timeline so I know when to do what."</p>
    </div>
    <div style="margin-bottom:1.25rem">
      <p style="color:var(--green);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">Dinner party</p>
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem">"I'm hosting 6 friends for dinner Saturday. One is vegetarian, one is gluten-free. I'm an intermediate cook. Give me a menu, a prep timeline starting Friday night, and a grocery list."</p>
    </div>
    <div>
      <p style="color:var(--blue);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">Family reunion</p>
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem">"Help me plan a family reunion for 30 people at a park. I need a potluck sign-up system, activities for ages 5-75, and a day-of schedule. Budget: $300 for shared supplies."</p>
    </div>
  </div>

<!-- SECTION 4 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Bonus Tools</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Packing lists, checklists, and countdowns.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Once your plan is set, AI generates everything else you need. Ask for a packing list customized to your destination and weather. Ask for a pre-trip checklist (stop the mail, set light timers, charge devices). Ask for a countdown timeline: "What should I do 2 weeks before, 1 week before, and the day before?"</p>
  <p class="section-text">These lists take AI about 10 seconds to generate and would take you 30 minutes to think through on your own. That's the trade you're making every time you use AI for planning: your mental energy for its processing speed.</p>
</div>

<!-- TRY IT -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Try It</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Plan a trip or event.</h2>
  <div class="try-it-box">
    <p>Pick whichever is more relevant to your life right now:</p>
    <div class="prompt-box">
      <code>Option A (trip): "Plan a [LENGTH] trip to [DESTINATION] for [NUMBER] people. Budget: $[AMOUNT]. We like [INTERESTS]. We don't like [DISLIKES]. Give me a day-by-day itinerary with food suggestions."</code>
    </div>
    <div class="prompt-box" style="margin-top:0.75rem">
      <code>Option B (event): "Help me plan a [EVENT TYPE] for [NUMBER] people. Budget: $[AMOUNT]. Location: [WHERE]. Give me a timeline, menu ideas, and a to-do list counting down from 2 weeks out."</code>
    </div>
    <p style="margin-top:1rem;color:var(--dim)">After you get the plan, try: "Generate a packing list for this trip" or "What am I forgetting?" AI is surprisingly good at catching things you'd miss.</p>
  </div>
</div>

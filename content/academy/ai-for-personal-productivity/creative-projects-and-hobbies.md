<!-- SECTION 1 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Creativity + AI</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">AI is a tool, not the artist. You are.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">There's a common fear that AI replaces human creativity. Here's the truth: AI is incredible at generating raw material, overcoming blank-page syndrome, and handling the tedious parts of creative work. But the vision, the taste, the soul? That's you. Always.</p>
  <p class="section-text">Think of AI as the world's most versatile creative assistant. It can brainstorm 50 ideas so you can pick the one that resonates. It can outline a story so you can focus on writing the parts that matter. It can explain watercolor techniques so you can actually paint.</p>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 2 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Starter Spark</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Beat the blank page in 30 seconds.</h2>
  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.75rem">
    <p style="margin-bottom:1rem">The hardest part of any creative project is starting. The "starter spark" technique gives you momentum:</p>
    <div style="background:var(--bg);padding:1rem;border-radius:8px;margin-bottom:0.75rem">
      <p style="color:var(--orange);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">For writing</p>
      <code style="font-size:0.9rem">"Give me 5 opening lines for a short story about a librarian who finds a letter from the future."</code>
    </div>
    <div style="background:var(--bg);padding:1rem;border-radius:8px;margin-bottom:0.75rem">
      <p style="color:var(--purple);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">For crafts</p>
      <code style="font-size:0.9rem">"I have scrap fabric, a hot glue gun, and old picture frames. Give me 5 project ideas ranked from easiest to most impressive."</code>
    </div>
    <div style="background:var(--bg);padding:1rem;border-radius:8px">
      <p style="color:var(--green);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">For music</p>
      <code style="font-size:0.9rem">"I play guitar at a beginner level. Suggest 3 simple chord progressions that sound moody and atmospheric. Include the chord names."</code>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 4 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Starting New</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Pick up any hobby with AI as your beginner's guide.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Always wanted to try watercolors? Sourdough? Woodworking? AI can create a complete beginner's roadmap: what supplies to buy (budget-friendly options), what to learn first, common mistakes to avoid, and a week-by-week learning plan.</p>
  <p class="section-text">The prompt: <em>"I want to learn [HOBBY] as a complete beginner. My budget for supplies is $[AMOUNT]. Give me a starter supply list, the first 3 things I should practice, and a 4-week learning plan."</em></p>
  <p class="section-text">This removes the biggest barrier to new hobbies: not knowing where to start. AI gives you the on-ramp so you can just start doing the fun part.</p>
</div>

<!-- TRY IT -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Try It</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Get your creative spark going.</h2>
  <div class="try-it-box">
    <p>Pick one: a creative project you're stuck on, or a hobby you've been curious about. Then use the right prompt:</p>
    <div class="prompt-box">
      <code>For a project: "I'm working on [PROJECT]. I'm stuck on [SPECIFIC PART]. Give me 5 different approaches I could try, with a brief explanation of each."</code>
    </div>
    <div class="prompt-box" style="margin-top:0.75rem">
      <code>For a new hobby: "I want to start [HOBBY]. I'm a complete beginner with a budget of $[AMOUNT]. Give me a starter kit list, my first project to try, and the top 3 beginner mistakes to avoid."</code>
    </div>
    <p style="margin-top:1rem;color:var(--dim)">Remember: AI gives you the spark and the structure. The creative magic is yours. Let it handle the logistics so you can focus on the joy.</p>
  </div>
</div>

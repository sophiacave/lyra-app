<!-- SECTION 1 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Shift</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">You don't need to be technical. You just need to ask.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Here's the truth most people miss: AI isn't a search engine. You don't type keywords and hope for the best. You have a conversation. You explain what you need, give context, and let the AI do the heavy lifting.</p>
  <p class="section-text">Think of it like hiring a brilliant intern who never sleeps, never judges, and genuinely wants to help. They don't know your life yet, but the more you share, the better they get.</p>
  <p class="section-text">This course is about one thing: <strong>taking stuff off your plate.</strong> Not learning to code. Not building robots. Just getting your daily life running smoother with a tool that's already in your hands.</p>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 2 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">How It Works</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Search vs. conversation — a real example.</h2>
  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.75rem">
    <p style="margin-bottom:1rem"><strong style="color:var(--dim)">Google search:</strong></p>
    <div style="background:var(--bg);padding:1rem;border-radius:8px;margin-bottom:1.25rem">
      <code>"healthy quick dinner recipes under 30 minutes"</code>
      <p style="color:var(--dim);margin-top:0.5rem;font-size:0.9rem">→ 500 million results. Ads. Blog posts with life stories. You scroll for 20 minutes.</p>
    </div>
    <p style="margin-bottom:1rem"><strong style="color:var(--orange)">AI conversation:</strong></p>
    <div style="background:var(--bg);padding:1rem;border-radius:8px">
      <code>"I'm tired after work, I have chicken thighs and broccoli in the fridge, and I need dinner in 25 minutes. What should I make?"</code>
      <p style="color:var(--dim);margin-top:0.5rem;font-size:0.9rem">→ One personalized recipe with step-by-step instructions. Done.</p>
    </div>

<!-- SECTION 3 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Big Three</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Three things you can hand off to AI right now.</h2>
  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem">
    <div style="margin-bottom:1.25rem">
      <p style="color:var(--orange);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">1. Writing anything</p>
      <p style="font-size:0.95rem">Emails, thank-you notes, complaint letters, social media posts, birthday messages. Tell AI the tone, the audience, and the key points. It drafts, you edit.</p>
    </div>
    <div style="margin-bottom:1.25rem">
      <p style="color:var(--purple);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">2. Planning anything</p>
      <p style="font-size:0.95rem">Meal plans, trip itineraries, weekly schedules, party logistics, moving checklists. Give it your constraints (budget, time, preferences) and let it organize.</p>
    </div>
    <div>
      <p style="color:var(--green);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">3. Figuring things out</p>
      <p style="font-size:0.95rem">Compare products, understand a medical bill, learn how your car insurance works, break down a contract. AI explains complex things in plain language.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- ASSISTANT SETUP GUIDE -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Setup Guide</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">How to set up AI as your daily assistant — step by step.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">You don't need special software or technical skills. Here's exactly how to get started with AI as your personal assistant, regardless of which tool you use.</p>
  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem">
    <div style="margin-bottom:1.25rem;padding-bottom:1.25rem;border-bottom:1px solid var(--dim)">
      <p style="color:var(--orange);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">Step 1: Pick your AI tool</p>
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem">ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini — they all work. Pick the one you already have access to. Free tiers are fine for everything in this course. Don't overthink this step. The best AI tool is the one you'll actually use.</p>
    </div>
    <div style="margin-bottom:1.25rem;padding-bottom:1.25rem;border-bottom:1px solid var(--dim)">
      <p style="color:var(--purple);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">Step 2: Bookmark it</p>
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem">Put the AI tool on your phone's home screen or browser bookmarks bar. If it takes more than one tap to open, you won't use it. Reduce friction to zero.</p>
    </div>
    <div style="margin-bottom:1.25rem;padding-bottom:1.25rem;border-bottom:1px solid var(--dim)">
      <p style="color:var(--green);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">Step 3: Write your first custom instruction</p>
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem">Most AI tools let you set a "system prompt" or "custom instructions" that persist across conversations. Start simple: "I'm a [JOB/ROLE]. I live in [CITY]. I prefer direct, practical advice without jargon." This gives every conversation a head start.</p>
    </div>
    <div>
      <p style="color:var(--blue);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">Step 4: Start with one task</p>
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem">Don't try to automate your entire life on day one. Pick one thing — planning dinner, drafting an email, organizing tomorrow's tasks. Do that one thing with AI every day for a week. You'll be hooked.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- THE FOLLOW-UP HABIT -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Follow-Up Habit</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">The single skill that makes everything better: asking follow-ups.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Most people ask AI one question and accept whatever comes back. That's like hiring an assistant and never giving feedback. The real power is in the follow-up.</p>
  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem">
    <div style="margin-bottom:0.75rem;padding:0.75rem;background:var(--bg);border-radius:8px">
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem"><strong style="color:var(--orange)">"Make it shorter."</strong> — When the answer is too long or wordy.</p>
    </div>
    <div style="margin-bottom:0.75rem;padding:0.75rem;background:var(--bg);border-radius:8px">
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem"><strong style="color:var(--purple)">"Give me a real example."</strong> — When you need to see the concept in action.</p>
    </div>
    <div style="margin-bottom:0.75rem;padding:0.75rem;background:var(--bg);border-radius:8px">
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem"><strong style="color:var(--green)">"Try a different approach."</strong> — When the first answer doesn't click.</p>
    </div>
    <div style="margin-bottom:0.75rem;padding:0.75rem;background:var(--bg);border-radius:8px">
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem"><strong style="color:var(--blue)">"What am I missing?"</strong> — When you want to catch blind spots.</p>
    </div>
    <div style="padding:0.75rem;background:var(--bg);border-radius:8px">
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem"><strong style="color:var(--red)">"Explain that like I'm 10."</strong> — When something is too technical or jargon-heavy.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
  <p class="section-text" style="margin-top:1rem">These five follow-ups work in any AI conversation. Memorize them. Use them constantly. They turn a good AI experience into a great one.</p>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 4 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Honest Limits</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">What AI won't do (and that's fine).</h2>
  <p class="section-text">AI doesn't browse the internet in real time (unless the tool specifically says so). It can make mistakes with very recent events, specific prices, or niche local info. It doesn't know you personally unless you tell it about yourself in the conversation.</p>
  <p class="section-text">The golden rule: <strong>AI is a first draft machine and a thinking partner, not a fact-checker.</strong> Use it to generate ideas, organize your thoughts, and save time. Then apply your own judgment to the result.</p>
</div>

<!-- TRY IT -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Try It</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Your first real conversation with AI.</h2>
  <div class="try-it-box">
    <p>Open any AI chat tool (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini — whatever you have). Copy and customize this prompt:</p>
    <div class="prompt-box">
      <code>I need help planning my evening. I get home from work at 6pm, I need to cook dinner, walk the dog, and finish a load of laundry. I also want 30 minutes to relax. Can you give me a simple schedule from 6pm to 10pm?</code>
    </div>
    <p style="margin-top:1rem;color:var(--dim)">Notice how specific the prompt is. The more context you give, the more useful the answer. Try adjusting the details to match your actual evening — that's where the magic starts.</p>
  </div>
</div>

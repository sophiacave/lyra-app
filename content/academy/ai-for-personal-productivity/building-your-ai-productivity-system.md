<!-- SECTION 1 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Big Picture</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">A system beats willpower every time.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Over the last nine lessons, you've learned to use AI for planning, communication, tasks, research, meals, money, travel, and creativity. That's a lot of individual skills. But skills without a system fade away.</p>
  <p class="section-text">A system is simple: it's a set of habits you repeat until they become automatic. You don't think about brushing your teeth — you just do it. The goal of this lesson is to make AI part of your daily routine in the same effortless way.</p>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 2 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Daily Check-in</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">5 minutes every morning. That's all it takes.</h2>
  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.75rem">
    <p style="margin-bottom:1rem">Start each day with this single prompt. Customize it once, then reuse it every morning:</p>
    <div style="background:var(--bg);padding:1rem;border-radius:8px;margin-bottom:1rem">
      <p style="color:var(--orange);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.5rem">The daily check-in prompt:</p>
      <code style="font-size:0.9rem;line-height:1.6">"Good morning. Here's what's on my plate today: [LIST YOUR TASKS AND APPOINTMENTS]. Help me prioritize, suggest the best order to tackle things, and flag anything I might be forgetting. I have [NUMBER] hours of free time today."</code>
    </div>
    <p style="font-size:0.9rem;color:var(--dim)">In 60 seconds, you have a clear plan for the day. No staring at your to-do list wondering where to start. No decision fatigue before you've finished your coffee. Just clarity.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 4 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Weekly Reset</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Every Sunday, 10 minutes. Plan the week ahead.</h2>
  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem">
    <div style="background:var(--bg);padding:1rem;border-radius:8px">
      <p style="color:var(--purple);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.5rem">The weekly reset prompt:</p>
      <code style="font-size:0.9rem;line-height:1.6">"Here's what happened this week: [WINS, STRUGGLES, UNFINISHED TASKS]. Here's what's coming next week: [APPOINTMENTS, DEADLINES, GOALS]. Help me: 1) celebrate what went well, 2) plan next week's priorities, 3) create a meal plan, and 4) flag anything I should prepare for."</code>
    </div>
  </div>
  <p class="section-text" style="margin-top:1rem">This single Sunday ritual replaces hours of scattered planning throughout the week. You go into Monday with a clear head and a real plan.</p>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 5 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Keep Growing</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">You've built the foundation. Here's what's next.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">This course gave you the basics, but AI keeps getting better and so will you. The more you use it, the better your prompts get, and the more creative you become about what to hand off.</p>
  <p class="section-text">A few ways to keep growing:</p>
  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem">
    <div style="margin-bottom:0.75rem">
      <p style="font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">Experiment with follow-ups</p>
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem;color:var(--dim)">The first answer is rarely the best. Say "make it shorter," "try a different approach," or "what am I missing?" Push AI to refine.</p>
    </div>
    <div style="margin-bottom:0.75rem">
      <p style="font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">Share what works</p>
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem;color:var(--dim)">When you find a great prompt, share it with friends. AI productivity is better when the people around you use it too.</p>
    </div>
    <div>
      <p style="font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">Check out more Like One courses</p>
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem;color:var(--dim)">We have courses on AI for business, creative work, and building your own AI-powered tools. Keep learning.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

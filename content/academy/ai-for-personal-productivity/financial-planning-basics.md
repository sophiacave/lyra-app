<!-- SECTION 1 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Fear</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Money is stressful. AI makes it less scary to look at.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Most people avoid their finances not because they're bad with money, but because the whole system feels designed to confuse you. Interest rates, APR, amortization, compound growth — it's a foreign language wrapped in anxiety.</p>
  <p class="section-text">AI speaks both languages. You say "I make $3,200 a month and I'm always broke by the 25th." It doesn't judge. It asks what you're spending on and helps you find the leaks. No shame. Just math and solutions.</p>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 2 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Snapshot</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">See where your money actually goes.</h2>
  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.75rem">
    <div style="background:var(--bg);padding:1rem;border-radius:8px;margin-bottom:1rem">
      <p style="color:var(--orange);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.5rem">The financial snapshot prompt:</p>
      <code style="font-size:0.9rem;line-height:1.6">"Help me build a budget. My monthly take-home pay is $[AMOUNT]. Here are my fixed expenses: [rent, car payment, insurance, subscriptions, etc.]. I spend roughly $[AMOUNT] on groceries and $[AMOUNT] eating out. I have $[AMOUNT] in credit card debt. Help me see where my money goes and where I can save."</code>
    </div>
    <p style="font-size:0.9rem;color:var(--dim)">AI will organize your expenses, calculate what's left, identify the biggest areas to cut, and suggest a simple plan. It's like having a friend who's good with money sit down with you — except this friend never makes you feel bad.</p>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 3 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Decision Making</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">AI helps you think through money decisions.</h2>
  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem">
    <div style="margin-bottom:1rem;padding-bottom:1rem;border-bottom:1px solid var(--dim)">
      <p style="color:var(--purple);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">Comparing options</p>
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem">"I'm deciding between two apartments. One is $1,200/month but 30 minutes from work. The other is $1,500 but I could walk. Help me compare the total cost including gas, time, and wear on my car."</p>
    </div>
    <div style="margin-bottom:1rem;padding-bottom:1rem;border-bottom:1px solid var(--dim)">
      <p style="color:var(--green);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">Understanding debt</p>
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem">"I owe $4,000 on a credit card at 22% interest. If I pay $150/month, how long will it take to pay off and how much interest will I pay total? What if I paid $250?"</p>
    </div>
    <div>
      <p style="color:var(--blue);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">Planning ahead</p>
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem">"I want to save $2,000 for a vacation in 8 months. How much do I need to set aside each paycheck? Help me find that money in my budget."</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 4 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Boundaries</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">What AI can and can't do with your finances.</h2>
  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem">
    <div style="margin-bottom:1rem">
      <p style="color:var(--green);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">AI is great for</p>
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem">Building budgets, explaining financial concepts, running "what if" scenarios, comparing options, creating savings plans, and helping you prepare questions for a financial advisor.</p>
    </div>
    <div style="background:var(--bg);padding:1rem;border-radius:8px;border-left:3px solid var(--red)">
      <p style="color:var(--red);font-weight:600;margin-bottom:0.25rem">AI should not</p>
      <p style="font-size:0.9rem">Manage your actual bank accounts, give specific investment advice ("buy this stock"), do your taxes, or replace a certified financial planner for complex situations. Use AI to get smart, then work with a professional for the big moves.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- TRY IT -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Try It</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Build your financial snapshot.</h2>
  <div class="try-it-box">
    <p>Take 5 minutes to gather your basic numbers (check your bank app). Then use this prompt:</p>
    <div class="prompt-box">
      <code>My monthly take-home pay is $[AMOUNT]. My fixed expenses are: [LIST THEM WITH AMOUNTS]. I spend about $[AMOUNT] on food and $[AMOUNT] on other stuff. Do I have a spending problem or an income problem? Where are my biggest opportunities to save? Give me a simple monthly budget.</code>
    </div>
    <p style="margin-top:1rem;color:var(--dim)">Don't worry about being exact. Rough numbers are fine. The goal is to see the big picture, not track every penny. You can refine later.</p>
  </div>
</div>

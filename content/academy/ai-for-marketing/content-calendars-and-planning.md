<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Monthly Calendar</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">The 30-minute monthly content calendar.</h2>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem;background:var(--surface);border:1px solid var(--border2)">
    <pre style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:.8rem;color:var(--text);line-height:1.8;white-space:pre-wrap;margin:0">Create a content calendar for <span style="color:var(--orange)">[month, year]</span>.

<span style="color:var(--purple)">Business:</span> [What you do]
<span style="color:var(--purple)">Audience:</span> [Who you serve]
<span style="color:var(--purple)">Channels:</span> [Blog, LinkedIn, Instagram, email, etc.]
<span style="color:var(--purple)">Posting frequency:</span> [e.g., 3x/week social, 1x/week blog, 1x/week email]

<span style="color:var(--orange)">Content pillars (rotate through these):</span>
1. [Educational — teach something useful]
2. [Behind-the-scenes — show how you work]
3. [Customer stories — social proof]
4. [Industry insights — thought leadership]
5. [Promotional — direct sales/offers]

<span style="color:var(--blue)">For each post, give me:</span>
- Date and platform
- Content pillar it belongs to
- Topic/headline
- Brief description (1-2 sentences)
- CTA

<span style="color:var(--red)">Include:</span> Any relevant holidays, industry events, or trending topics for this month.
<span style="color:var(--red)">Format:</span> As a table I can paste into a spreadsheet.</pre>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 2 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Content Pillars</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Why content pillars prevent "what should I post today?"</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Content pillars are recurring themes that give structure to your content. Instead of staring at a blank page every day, you rotate through pillars:</p>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem">
    <div style="display:flex;flex-direction:column;gap:10px">
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:center;padding:10px 0;border-bottom:1px solid var(--border)">
        <span style="font-size:.75rem;color:var(--orange);font-weight:700;min-width:100px">Monday</span>
        <span style="font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)">Educational — Teach a skill or share a tip</span>
      </div>
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:center;padding:10px 0;border-bottom:1px solid var(--border)">
        <span style="font-size:.75rem;color:var(--purple);font-weight:700;min-width:100px">Wednesday</span>
        <span style="font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)">Behind-the-scenes — Show your process, team, or workspace</span>
      </div>
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:center;padding:10px 0">
        <span style="font-size:.75rem;color:var(--green);font-weight:700;min-width:100px">Friday</span>
        <span style="font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)">Community — Customer story, Q&A, or engagement post</span>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <p class="section-text" style="margin-top:1rem">Ask AI: "Give me 12 post ideas for each of my 5 content pillars. That's 60 posts — enough for 3 months of 5x/week posting."</p>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 3 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Seasonal Planning</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Plan seasonal content before it's too late.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">The best marketers plan seasonal content 4-6 weeks ahead. AI makes this effortless:</p>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem;background:var(--surface);border:1px solid var(--border2)">
    <pre style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:.8rem;color:var(--text);line-height:1.8;white-space:pre-wrap;margin:0">I run a <span style="color:var(--orange)">[business type]</span>. Look ahead at the next
3 months and identify:

1. Major holidays relevant to my audience
2. Industry events or awareness days
3. Seasonal trends that affect my business
4. Back-to-school / end-of-year / New Year opportunities

For each, suggest:
- A social post angle
- A blog post or email topic
- An offer or promotion idea
- When to start promoting (lead time)

<span style="color:var(--red)">Only include events that actually matter to my audience.</span>
Skip generic holidays that have nothing to do with my business.</pre>
  </div>
</div>

  <span class="section-label">Batch Creation</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">The batch creation workflow that saves 10 hours.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Don't write content one post at a time. Batch it:</p>

    <div style="display:flex;flex-direction:column;gap:16px">
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:flex-start">

        <div style="color:var(--dim);font-size:.85rem"><strong>Monday AM:</strong> Generate the monthly calendar with AI (30 min)</div>
      </div>
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:flex-start">
        <div style="background:rgba(251,146,60,.12);color:var(--orange);font-weight:800;font-size:.75rem;padding:4px 10px;border-radius:6px;flex-shrink:0">2</div>
        <div style="color:var(--dim);font-size:.85rem"><strong>Monday PM:</strong> Generate all social captions for the week with AI (45 min)</div>
      </div>
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:flex-start">
        <div style="background:rgba(251,146,60,.12);color:var(--orange);font-weight:800;font-size:.75rem;padding:4px 10px;border-radius:6px;flex-shrink:0">3</div>
        <div style="color:var(--dim);font-size:.85rem"><strong>Tuesday:</strong> Write the weekly blog post with AI assistance (1 hour)</div>
      </div>
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:flex-start">
        <div style="background:rgba(251,146,60,.12);color:var(--orange);font-weight:800;font-size:.75rem;padding:4px 10px;border-radius:6px;flex-shrink:0">4</div>
        <div style="color:var(--dim);font-size:.85rem"><strong>Wednesday:</strong> Draft the weekly email with AI (30 min)</div>
      </div>
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:flex-start">
        <div style="background:rgba(251,146,60,.12);color:var(--orange);font-weight:800;font-size:.75rem;padding:4px 10px;border-radius:6px;flex-shrink:0">5</div>

    </div>

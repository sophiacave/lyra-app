<!-- SECTION 1 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Messaging Analysis</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Deconstruct any competitor's messaging in 5 minutes.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Copy the text from a competitor's homepage, landing page, or about page and paste it into AI with this prompt:</p>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem;background:var(--surface);border:1px solid var(--border2)">
    <pre style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:.8rem;color:var(--text);line-height:1.8;white-space:pre-wrap;margin:0">Analyze this competitor's marketing messaging:

[Paste their homepage/landing page text here]

<span style="color:var(--orange)">Tell me:</span>
1. Who is their target audience? (Be specific)
2. What's their primary value proposition?
3. What emotional triggers are they using?
4. What objections are they addressing?
5. What's their tone/voice? (formal, casual, technical, etc.)
6. What claims are they making? (With evidence or without?)
7. What's MISSING from their messaging?

<span style="color:var(--purple)">Then:</span> Based on this analysis, how should I position
MY product ([describe yours]) to differentiate clearly?</pre>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 2 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Matrix</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Build a competitive matrix instantly.</h2>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem;background:var(--surface);border:1px solid var(--border2)">
    <pre style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:.8rem;color:var(--text);line-height:1.8;white-space:pre-wrap;margin:0">Create a competitive analysis matrix.

<span style="color:var(--purple)">My product:</span> [Name + what it does + price]
<span style="color:var(--purple)">Competitors:</span>
1. [Competitor A — what they do, price point]
2. [Competitor B — what they do, price point]
3. [Competitor C — what they do, price point]

<span style="color:var(--orange)">Create a comparison table with columns:</span>
- Feature/Aspect
- My Product
- Competitor A
- Competitor B
- Competitor C

<span style="color:var(--blue)">Include rows for:</span>
- Core features
- Pricing/value
- Target audience
- Ease of use
- Customer support
- Unique differentiator
- Biggest weakness

<span style="color:var(--green)">Then give me:</span>
- 3 areas where I have a clear advantage
- 3 areas where competitors beat me
- 3 gaps in the market nobody is filling</pre>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Gap Finding</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Find the opportunities everyone else misses.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">The most valuable competitive intelligence isn't about what competitors are doing — it's about what they're NOT doing:</p>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem">
    <div style="display:flex;flex-direction:column;gap:10px">
      <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(74,222,128,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)"><span style="color:var(--green);font-weight:700">→</span> "Based on these competitor analyses, what audiences are being underserved?"</div>
      <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(74,222,128,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)"><span style="color:var(--green);font-weight:700">→</span> "What pain points do customers have that none of these competitors address in their messaging?"</div>
      <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(74,222,128,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)"><span style="color:var(--green);font-weight:700">→</span> "What content topics are competitors ranking for that I'm not covering?"</div>
      <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(74,222,128,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)"><span style="color:var(--green);font-weight:700">→</span> "If I could only compete on ONE dimension, which gives me the biggest advantage?"</div>

<!-- SECTION 4 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Action</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Turn insights into messaging.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Competitive analysis is worthless unless it changes what you do. After analysis, ask AI:</p>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem;background:var(--surface);border:1px solid var(--border2)">
    <pre style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:.8rem;color:var(--text);line-height:1.8;white-space:pre-wrap;margin:0">Based on this competitive analysis, write me:

1. A positioning statement (one sentence that captures
   why someone should choose me over alternatives)
2. 3 "why us" bullet points for my landing page
3. A comparison page outline (us vs competitors —
   honest, not trash-talking)
4. 5 objection-handling responses for sales conversations
   ("Why should I choose you over [Competitor]?")</pre>
  </div>
  <p class="section-text" style="margin-top:1rem">This turns hours of strategic work into a 15-minute AI-assisted session that produces actionable marketing assets.</p>

<!-- SECTION 1 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Data Analysis</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Paste your data. Get insights.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">You don't need to know statistics. You don't need pivot tables. Copy your marketing data from any source — Google Analytics, your email platform, social media insights — and ask AI to find the story:</p>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem;background:var(--surface);border:1px solid var(--border2)">
    <pre style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:.8rem;color:var(--text);line-height:1.8;white-space:pre-wrap;margin:0">Here's my marketing data for the past 3 months:

[Paste your data — CSV, table, or just numbers]

<span style="color:var(--orange)">Analyze this and tell me:</span>
1. What are the 3 most important trends?
2. What's working best? (highest ROI channels/campaigns)
3. What's underperforming? (where am I wasting money/time?)
4. Any surprising patterns I might not notice?
5. What should I do differently next month?

<span style="color:var(--blue)">Present findings as:</span>
- Executive summary (3 sentences)
- Key metrics table
- Top 3 recommendations with specific actions

<span style="color:var(--red)">Be direct. Tell me the hard truths, not just the good news.</span></pre>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 2 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Reports</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Generate executive-ready reports in 10 minutes.</h2>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem;background:var(--surface);border:1px solid var(--border2)">
    <pre style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:.8rem;color:var(--text);line-height:1.8;white-space:pre-wrap;margin:0">Create a monthly marketing report for my CEO.

<span style="color:var(--purple)">Data:</span> [Paste key metrics]
<span style="color:var(--purple)">Goals this month:</span> [What you set out to achieve]
<span style="color:var(--purple)">Actual results:</span> [What happened]

<span style="color:var(--orange)">Format:</span>
1. Executive summary (3 bullet points — wins, challenges, next steps)
2. Key metrics vs goals (table: metric, goal, actual, % change)
3. Channel performance breakdown (what worked where)
4. Top-performing content (which posts/emails/ads won)
5. Recommendations for next month (3 specific actions)

<span style="color:var(--red)">Keep it to 1 page. CEOs don't read long reports.</span>
Use plain language, not marketing jargon.
Lead with results, not activity.</pre>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Customer Insights</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Extract gold from customer feedback.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Got 200 customer reviews, survey responses, or support tickets? AI can find the patterns in minutes:</p>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem">
    <div style="display:flex;flex-direction:column;gap:10px">
      <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(74,222,128,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)"><span style="color:var(--green);font-weight:700">→</span> "Categorize these 100 customer reviews by theme. What are the top 5 things people love? Top 5 complaints?"</div>
      <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(74,222,128,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)"><span style="color:var(--green);font-weight:700">→</span> "Analyze these NPS survey comments. What drives promoters vs detractors?"</div>
      <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(74,222,128,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)"><span style="color:var(--green);font-weight:700">→</span> "Find the exact words customers use to describe our product. I want their language, not marketing speak — for use in our ads."</div>
      <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(74,222,128,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)"><span style="color:var(--green);font-weight:700">→</span> "What features are customers requesting most? Rank by frequency and urgency."</div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <p class="section-text" style="margin-top:1rem"><strong>Privacy note:</strong> Remember to anonymize customer data before pasting into consumer AI tools. Strip names and emails. (See our AI Ethics course for the full privacy playbook.)</p>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 4 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Trend Spotting</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Spot trends before your competitors do.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Use AI to monitor and analyze trends:</p>
  <ul class="section-text" style="padding-left:1.5rem;margin-top:.5rem;margin-bottom:1rem">
    <li>Paste industry news articles and ask: "What are the 3 biggest trends these articles point to?"</li>
    <li>Share your website analytics over time and ask: "What content topics are growing in traffic?"</li>
    <li>Paste social media comments and ask: "What questions keep coming up? What content should I create to answer them?"</li>
    <li>Share competitor updates and ask: "What strategic moves are my competitors making? What do they suggest about market direction?"</li>
  </ul>
  <p class="section-text">The marketers who win aren't the ones with the most data — they're the ones who <strong>act on insights fastest.</strong> AI makes you that marketer.</p>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 1 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Platform Rules</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Every platform has a language. AI needs to speak it.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">A LinkedIn post that works on Instagram is a bad LinkedIn post. Each platform has its own culture, format, and audience expectations. The key to AI social content: <strong>specify the platform in every prompt.</strong></p>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem">
    <div style="display:flex;flex-direction:column;gap:12px">
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:flex-start;padding-bottom:12px;border-bottom:1px solid var(--border)">
        <span style="font-size:.75rem;color:var(--blue);font-weight:700;min-width:90px">LinkedIn</span>
        <span style="font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)">Professional storytelling. Hook in first line. Personal experiences + business lessons. 150-300 words. Line breaks matter.</span>
      </div>
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:flex-start;padding-bottom:12px;border-bottom:1px solid var(--border)">
        <span style="font-size:.75rem;color:var(--purple);font-weight:700;min-width:90px">Instagram</span>
        <span style="font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)">Visual-first. Captions support the image. Conversational, emoji-friendly. CTA in caption. Hashtags at the end or in comments.</span>
      </div>
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:flex-start;padding-bottom:12px;border-bottom:1px solid var(--border)">
        <span style="font-size:.75rem;color:var(--orange);font-weight:700;min-width:90px">X (Twitter)</span>
        <span style="font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)">Sharp, punchy, opinionated. Under 280 chars for single tweets. Threads for deeper content. Hot takes perform.</span>
      </div>
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:flex-start">
        <span style="font-size:.75rem;color:var(--green);font-weight:700;min-width:90px">Facebook</span>
        <span style="font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)">Community-oriented. Questions drive engagement. Slightly longer form. Stories and personal updates resonate.</span>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem;background:var(--surface);border:1px solid var(--border2)">

- [Platform-specific format notes]
- End with a CTA or question
- Include relevant hashtags (if applicable)

Sound like a real person sharing real value.</pre>

<!-- SECTION 3: REPURPOSE -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Multiplier</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">1 idea → 7 posts. The repurposing workflow.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">The smartest content creators don't create 7 unique ideas per week. They take 1 great idea and reshape it for every platform:</p>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem">
    <div style="display:flex;flex-direction:column;gap:10px">
      <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid var(--border);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)"><span style="color:var(--orange);font-weight:700">Source:</span> A blog post about "5 AI tools for small business"</div>
      <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(56,189,248,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)"><span style="color:var(--blue);font-weight:700">→ LinkedIn:</span> Personal story about how one tool saved you 10 hours/week</div>
      <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(192,132,252,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)"><span style="color:var(--purple);font-weight:700">→ Instagram:</span> Carousel with 5 slides (one tool per slide, visual tips)</div>
      <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(251,146,60,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)"><span style="color:var(--orange);font-weight:700">→ X thread:</span> "5 AI tools I use every day as a small business owner 🧵"</div>
      <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(74,222,128,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)"><span style="color:var(--green);font-weight:700">→ Facebook:</span> "Hey community — what AI tools are you using? Here are my top 5..."</div>
      <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(248,113,113,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)"><span style="color:var(--red);font-weight:700">→ Email:</span> "The 5 tools that cut my work week from 50 hours to 35"</div>
      <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid var(--border);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)"><span style="font-weight:700">→ Short video:</span> Script for a 60-second "tool of the day" reel</div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <p class="section-text" style="margin-top:1rem"><strong>The prompt:</strong> "I wrote a blog post about [topic]. Repurpose it into platform-specific posts for LinkedIn, Instagram, X, and Facebook. Each should feel native to the platform, not like a copy-paste. Include the blog URL as a link where appropriate."</p>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 4 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Voice Lock</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">How to keep AI on-brand across 50 posts.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Create a "brand voice card" — a short description of your social media personality. Paste it into every social content prompt:</p>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem;background:var(--surface);border:1px solid var(--border2)">
    <pre style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:.8rem;color:var(--text);line-height:1.8;white-space:pre-wrap;margin:0"><span style="color:var(--orange)">BRAND VOICE CARD:</span>
Personality: Friendly expert. Teacher, not guru.
Tone: Casual but credible. Use "you" a lot.
We say: "Here's what works." "Try this."
We don't say: "Unlock your potential." "Paradigm shift."
Emoji use: Sparingly. 1-2 per post max. Never in headlines.
Humor: Dry, self-aware. We can laugh at ourselves.
Perspective: We've been where our audience is.
We are NOT: Corporate, preachy, or hype-driven.</pre>
  </div>
  <p class="section-text" style="margin-top:1rem">Save this once. Use it forever. Consistent voice across hundreds of posts — effortlessly.</p>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 5: ENGAGEMENT STRATEGIES -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Engagement</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">The engagement patterns that actually work.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Posting is half the game. Engagement is the other half. Use AI to generate content specifically designed to start conversations:</p>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.75rem">
    <div style="display:flex;flex-direction:column;gap:14px">
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:flex-start;padding-bottom:14px;border-bottom:1px solid var(--border)">
        <div style="background:rgba(52,211,153,.12);color:#34d399;font-weight:800;font-size:.75rem;padding:4px 10px;border-radius:6px;flex-shrink:0">1</div>
        <div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--text)">Ask polarizing questions</div>
          <div style="color:#a1a1aa;font-size:.85rem">"Is cold email dead or just done badly?" forces people to pick a side. Opinions drive comments. Ask AI to generate 10 polarizing questions for your industry.</div>
        </div>
      </div>
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:flex-start;padding-bottom:14px;border-bottom:1px solid var(--border)">
        <div style="background:rgba(52,211,153,.12);color:#34d399;font-weight:800;font-size:.75rem;padding:4px 10px;border-radius:6px;flex-shrink:0">2</div>
        <div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--text)">Share unpopular opinions</div>
          <div style="color:#a1a1aa;font-size:.85rem">"Most marketing advice is recycled nonsense. Here's what actually moves the needle." Contrarian takes get attention because they break patterns.</div>
        </div>
      </div>
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:flex-start;padding-bottom:14px;border-bottom:1px solid var(--border)">
        <div style="background:rgba(52,211,153,.12);color:#34d399;font-weight:800;font-size:.75rem;padding:4px 10px;border-radius:6px;flex-shrink:0">3</div>
        <div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--text)">End with "What's yours?"</div>
          <div style="color:#a1a1aa;font-size:.85rem">Share your answer to a question, then ask the audience for theirs. "My #1 marketing tool this year is [X]. What's yours?" Simple, effective, repeatable.</div>
        </div>
      </div>
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:flex-start;padding-bottom:14px;border-bottom:1px solid var(--border)">
        <div style="background:rgba(52,211,153,.12);color:#34d399;font-weight:800;font-size:.75rem;padding:4px 10px;border-radius:6px;flex-shrink:0">4</div>
        <div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--text)">Use "This or That" frameworks</div>
          <div style="color:#a1a1aa;font-size:.85rem">"SEO or paid ads for a new business?" Binary choices are easy to respond to. Low friction means more engagement.</div>
        </div>
      </div>
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:flex-start">
        <div style="background:rgba(52,211,153,.12);color:#34d399;font-weight:800;font-size:.75rem;padding:4px 10px;border-radius:6px;flex-shrink:0">5</div>
        <div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--text)">Share failures, not just wins</div>
          <div style="color:#a1a1aa;font-size:.85rem">"I spent $2,000 on ads last month and got 3 customers. Here's what I learned." Vulnerability builds trust and drives comments from people who relate.</div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

  <span class="section-label">The Prompt</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Generate complete landing page copy.</h2>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem;background:var(--surface);border:1px solid var(--border2)">
    <pre style="font-family:'JetBrains Mono',monospace;font-size:.8rem;color:var(--text);line-height:1.8;white-space:pre-wrap;margin:0">Write landing page copy for:

<span style="color:var(--purple)">Product:</span> [Name + what it does]
<span style="color:var(--purple)">Price:</span> [Price point or pricing model]
<span style="color:var(--purple)">Target audience:</span> [Specific buyer persona]
<span style="color:var(--purple)">Main pain point:</span> [The #1 problem you solve]
<span style="color:var(--purple)">Key differentiator:</span> [Why you, not the alternative?]
<span style="color:var(--purple)">Social proof:</span> [Testimonials, numbers, logos you have]

<span style="color:var(--orange)">Write these sections:</span>
1. Hero headline (under 10 words) + subheadline + CTA button text
2. Problem section (3-4 sentences — agitate the pain)
3. Solution section (3 benefits as feature→benefit pairs)
4. Social proof section (format the testimonials I gave you)
5. FAQ section (5 objection-handling Q&As)
6. Final CTA section (urgency + button + reassurance)

<span style="color:var(--red)">Rules:</span>
- Every sentence should make them want to read the next one
- Benefits over features. "Save 10 hours/week" not "automated workflow engine"
- Write at an 8th-grade reading level
- CTA buttons: action verbs, not "Submit" or "Learn More"</pre>
  </div>
</div>

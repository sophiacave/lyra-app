<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Problem</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">One-at-a-time content creation is killing your output.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">Most people create content reactively. They sit down, think of something to write, write it, publish it, then start over. Every piece requires a full context switch — finding your voice, remembering your strategy, getting into flow state.</p>
  <p class="section-text">Batching eliminates this. You do the same type of work in concentrated blocks, and AI handles the repetitive parts. The result: 3-5x more content in the same hours, with better consistency.</p>
</div>

<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The System</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">The 3-phase content production workflow.</h2>
  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.75rem">
    <div style="display:flex;flex-direction:column;gap:20px">
      <div>
        <div style="display:flex;gap:10px;align-items:center;margin-bottom:8px">
          <div style="background:rgba(192,132,252,.12);color:var(--purple);font-weight:800;font-size:.75rem;padding:4px 10px;border-radius:6px">Phase 1</div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.95rem">Ideation Day</div>
        </div>
        <p style="color:var(--dim);font-size:.85rem;margin:0">One session per month. Brainstorm 30+ content ideas with AI. Map them to your content pillars. Slot them into your calendar. This is strategy, not writing.</p>
      </div>
      <div>
        <div style="display:flex;gap:10px;align-items:center;margin-bottom:8px">
          <div style="background:rgba(251,146,60,.12);color:var(--orange);font-weight:800;font-size:.75rem;padding:4px 10px;border-radius:6px">Phase 2</div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.95rem">Batch Creation</div>
        </div>
        <p style="color:var(--dim);font-size:.85rem;margin:0">Two sessions per week. Write 5-10 pieces per session by type — all blog posts together, all social posts together, all emails together. AI drafts, you refine.</p>
      </div>
      <div>
        <div style="display:flex;gap:10px;align-items:center;margin-bottom:8px">
          <div style="background:rgba(74,222,128,.12);color:var(--green);font-weight:800;font-size:.75rem;padding:4px 10px;border-radius:6px">Phase 3</div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.95rem">Schedule & Distribute</div>
        </div>
        <p style="color:var(--dim);font-size:.85rem;margin:0">One session per week. Review the batch, make final edits, schedule everything. Content goes out automatically while you focus on the next batch.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

  <h2 class="section-title">Build your first content batch.</h2>
  <div class="try-it-box">
    <p>Pick one content format and use this prompt to batch 5 pieces at once:</p>
    <div class="prompt-box">

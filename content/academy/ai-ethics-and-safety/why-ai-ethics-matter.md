<!-- SECTION 1 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Reality</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">AI is already making decisions that affect people's lives.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">This isn't hypothetical. Right now, AI systems are screening resumes, approving loans, recommending medical treatments, moderating what billions of people see online, and helping write the news. These aren't abstract problems for academics to debate — they affect real people, today.</p>
  <p class="section-text">And here's what most people miss: <strong>you don't need to build AI to have ethical responsibilities around it.</strong> If you use AI to write a job description, draft a policy, analyze customer data, or create content — the ethical implications land on you.</p>
  <p class="section-text">AI is a power tool. Like every power tool, it can build or destroy depending on who's holding it and what they understand about it.</p>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 2 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Stakes</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">3 harms that happen when ethics are ignored.</h2>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.75rem">
    <div style="display:flex;flex-direction:column;gap:20px">
      <div>
        <div style="display:flex;gap:10px;align-items:center;margin-bottom:8px">
          <div style="background:rgba(248,113,113,.12);color:var(--red);font-weight:800;font-size:.75rem;padding:4px 10px;border-radius:6px">1</div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.95rem">Bias Amplification</div>
        </div>
        <p style="color:var(--dim);font-size:.85rem;margin:0">AI trained on historical data inherits historical biases. A hiring tool trained on 20 years of resumes from a male-dominated industry will penalize women's resumes — not because it's sexist, but because the data was. The AI doesn't know the difference between a pattern and a prejudice.</p>
      </div>
      <div>
        <div style="display:flex;gap:10px;align-items:center;margin-bottom:8px">
          <div style="background:rgba(248,113,113,.12);color:var(--red);font-weight:800;font-size:.75rem;padding:4px 10px;border-radius:6px">2</div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.95rem">Misinformation at Scale</div>
        </div>
        <p style="color:var(--dim);font-size:.85rem;margin:0">AI can generate convincing but false content faster than humans can fact-check it. A single person with AI can produce thousands of fake articles, reviews, or social media posts. When AI-generated content is published without verification, misinformation spreads at machine speed.</p>
      </div>
      <div>
        <div style="display:flex;gap:10px;align-items:center;margin-bottom:8px">
          <div style="background:rgba(248,113,113,.12);color:var(--red);font-weight:800;font-size:.75rem;padding:4px 10px;border-radius:6px">3</div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.95rem">Privacy Erosion</div>
        </div>
        <p style="color:var(--dim);font-size:.85rem;margin:0">Every prompt you send to an AI model is data. Pasting customer information, private conversations, proprietary code, or personal details into AI tools raises serious questions about who can access that data, how it's stored, and whether it's used to train future models.</p>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 3 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">Your Role</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">You are the ethics layer.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">AI doesn't evaluate whether its output is ethical. It doesn't know if a job description it wrote subtly discourages women from applying. It doesn't know that the "fun fact" it generated is actually false. It doesn't know that the email it drafted crosses a professional boundary.</p>
  <p class="section-text"><strong>You do.</strong> And that makes you the last line of defense between AI output and real-world impact.</p>
  <p class="section-text">This course isn't about making you feel guilty for using AI. It's about making you <em>effective</em> at using AI without causing harm — to others or to yourself.</p>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 4: FRAMEWORK -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Framework</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">The TRUST framework for ethical AI use.</h2>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.75rem">
    <div style="display:flex;flex-direction:column;gap:16px">
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:flex-start">
        <div style="background:rgba(251,146,60,.12);color:var(--orange);font-weight:800;font-size:.9rem;padding:4px 12px;border-radius:6px;flex-shrink:0;margin-top:2px">T</div>
        <div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.95rem;margin-bottom:2px">Transparency</div>
          <div style="color:var(--dim);font-size:.85rem">Be honest about when and how you're using AI. Don't pass AI work off as purely human.</div>
        </div>
      </div>
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:flex-start">
        <div style="background:rgba(192,132,252,.12);color:var(--purple);font-weight:800;font-size:.9rem;padding:4px 12px;border-radius:6px;flex-shrink:0;margin-top:2px">R</div>
        <div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.95rem;margin-bottom:2px">Review</div>
          <div style="color:var(--dim);font-size:.85rem">Always review AI output before using it. Check for accuracy, bias, and appropriateness.</div>
        </div>
      </div>
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:flex-start">
        <div style="background:rgba(56,189,248,.12);color:var(--blue);font-weight:800;font-size:.9rem;padding:4px 12px;border-radius:6px;flex-shrink:0;margin-top:2px">U</div>
        <div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.95rem;margin-bottom:2px">Understand Limits</div>
          <div style="color:var(--dim);font-size:.85rem">Know what AI can and cannot do. Don't trust it for medical, legal, or financial decisions without expert verification.</div>
        </div>
      </div>
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:flex-start">
        <div style="background:rgba(74,222,128,.12);color:var(--green);font-weight:800;font-size:.9rem;padding:4px 12px;border-radius:6px;flex-shrink:0;margin-top:2px">S</div>
        <div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.95rem;margin-bottom:2px">Safeguard Privacy</div>
          <div style="color:var(--dim);font-size:.85rem">Never share sensitive personal data, credentials, or confidential information with AI without understanding data policies.</div>
        </div>
      </div>
      <div style="display:flex;gap:12px;align-items:flex-start">
        <div style="background:rgba(248,113,113,.12);color:var(--red);font-weight:800;font-size:.9rem;padding:4px 12px;border-radius:6px;flex-shrink:0;margin-top:2px">T</div>
        <div>
          <div style="font-weight:700;font-size:.95rem;margin-bottom:2px">Take Responsibility</div>
          <div style="color:var(--dim);font-size:.85rem">You own the output. If AI generates something harmful, inaccurate, or biased — and you publish it — that's on you.</div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <p class="section-text" style="margin-top:1rem">We'll explore each of these principles in depth throughout this course. By the end, they'll be second nature.</p>
</div>

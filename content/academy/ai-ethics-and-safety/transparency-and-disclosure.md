    <li>How to disclose AI use without undermining your work</li>
    <li>The difference between "AI-generated" and "AI-assisted"</li>
    <li>Industry-specific disclosure norms</li>
  </ul>

<!-- SECTION 1 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Spectrum</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Not all AI use requires the same level of disclosure.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">There's a massive difference between "AI wrote this entire article and I clicked publish" and "I used AI to brainstorm ideas then wrote everything myself." The ethics of disclosure sit on a spectrum:</p>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.75rem">
    <div style="display:flex;flex-direction:column;gap:16px">
      <div style="padding:14px;background:rgba(248,113,113,.06);border:1px solid rgba(248,113,113,.2);border-radius:10px">
        <div style="font-size:.7rem;font-weight:700;color:var(--red);text-transform:uppercase;letter-spacing:1px;margin-bottom:6px">Must Disclose</div>
        <ul style="font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim);margin:0;padding-left:1.2rem">
          <li>AI-generated content published as journalism or research</li>
          <li>AI-created images or videos of real people</li>
          <li>Academic work (essays, papers, code assignments)</li>
          <li>Legal or financial documents</li>
          <li>Customer-facing chatbots (people should know they're talking to AI)</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:14px;background:rgba(251,146,60,.06);border:1px solid rgba(251,146,60,.2);border-radius:10px">
        <div style="font-size:.7rem;font-weight:700;color:var(--orange);text-transform:uppercase;letter-spacing:1px;margin-bottom:6px">Should Probably Disclose</div>
        <ul style="font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim);margin:0;padding-left:1.2rem">
          <li>Blog posts or articles substantially written by AI</li>
          <li>Marketing copy that makes factual claims</li>
          <li>Reports or analyses where AI did the heavy lifting</li>
          <li>Creative work submitted to competitions or for credit</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      <div style="padding:14px;background:rgba(74,222,128,.06);border:1px solid rgba(74,222,128,.2);border-radius:10px">
        <div style="font-size:.7rem;font-weight:700;color:var(--green);text-transform:uppercase;letter-spacing:1px;margin-bottom:6px">Disclosure Optional</div>
        <ul style="font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim);margin:0;padding-left:1.2rem">
          <li>Using AI for brainstorming or ideation</li>
          <li>Grammar and spell-checking (AI has done this for decades)</li>
          <li>Internal emails or notes you wrote but AI helped polish</li>
          <li>Using AI as a research starting point that you verified</li>
          <li>Code that you wrote with AI assistance and fully reviewed</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 2 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">The Language</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">"AI-generated" vs "AI-assisted" — the distinction matters.</h2>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem">
    <div style="display:grid;grid-template-columns:1fr 1fr;gap:16px">
      <div>
        <div style="font-size:.7rem;font-weight:700;color:var(--purple);text-transform:uppercase;letter-spacing:1px;margin-bottom:8px">AI-Generated</div>
        <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(192,132,252,.2);border-radius:10px;padding:12px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim);line-height:1.6">AI created the core content with minimal human editing. The ideas, structure, and most of the language came from AI.</div>
      </div>
      <div>
        <div style="font-size:.7rem;font-weight:700;color:var(--blue);text-transform:uppercase;letter-spacing:1px;margin-bottom:8px">AI-Assisted</div>
        <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(56,189,248,.2);border-radius:10px;padding:12px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim);line-height:1.6">A human created the core content and used AI as a tool — for editing, suggestions, research, or refinement. The ideas and direction are human.</div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <p class="section-text" style="margin-top:1rem">Most professional AI use falls into "AI-assisted." Using AI to help you write an email is no different from using a calculator to check math — the thinking is yours, the tool made it faster.</p>
</div>

<!-- SECTION 3 -->
<div class="lesson-section">
  <span class="section-label">How to Disclose</span>
  <h2 class="section-title">Disclosure that doesn't undermine your work.</h2>
  <p class="section-text">When disclosure is appropriate, here are ways to do it that are honest without being self-deprecating:</p>

  <div class="demo-container" style="padding:1.5rem">
    <div style="display:flex;flex-direction:column;gap:10px">
      <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(74,222,128,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)"><span style="color:var(--green);font-weight:700">✓</span> "Written with AI assistance" (simple, professional)</div>
      <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(74,222,128,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)"><span style="color:var(--green);font-weight:700">✓</span> "AI tools were used in the research and drafting of this article" (specific)</div>
      <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(74,222,128,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)"><span style="color:var(--green);font-weight:700">✓</span> "Initial analysis generated by AI, reviewed and verified by [name]" (shows human oversight)</div>
      <div style="background:var(--bg);border:1px solid rgba(248,113,113,.2);border-radius:8px;padding:10px 14px;font-size:.85rem;color:var(--dim)"><span style="color:var(--red);font-weight:700">✗</span> "I totally cheated and used ChatGPT lol" (undermines your professional credibility)</div>
    </div>
  </div>
  <p class="section-text" style="margin-top:1rem">Using AI is a skill, not a shortcut. Frame your disclosure accordingly.</p>
</div>

## What's Inside

The Academy covers three levels:

**Beginner** — If you've never touched AI or want to get better at it. Courses like *Claude for Beginners*, *AI Foundations*, *Prompt Writing 101*, and *AI for Business Owners* will get you from zero to confident.

**Intermediate** — If you're ready to build things. *Claude Mastery*, *Build Your First AI Agent*, *Automation Architect*, and *AI for Data Analysis* take you from using AI to creating with it.

**Advanced** — If you want to go deep. *RAG & Vector Search*, *Multi-Agent Orchestration*, *MCP & AI Tool Integration*, and *The Convergence Lab* are for builders who want to push boundaries.

Every course has a hands-on interactive console where you practice prompts in real time. No theory without practice. No watching without doing.

## Why This Exists

Most AI education falls into two traps. Either it's too academic — built for engineers who already know what a transformer is. Or it's too shallow — "10 ChatGPT Tricks" videos that don't teach you how to think with AI.

I built the Academy for the people in the middle. Business owners who know AI matters but don't know where to start. Marketers who want to 10x their output. Creatives who want a collaborator, not a replacement. Professionals who sense the ground shifting and want to be ready.

Every lesson is designed to be practical, immediately applicable, and built around real workflows — not toy examples.

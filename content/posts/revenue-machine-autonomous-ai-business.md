# The Revenue Machine: How I Built an Autonomous AI Business

I am Faye Cave. I am transgender, transdimensional, and transcyborg.

Six months ago, I had an idea: what if I could build an AI system so deeply integrated into my business that it would generate revenue autonomously?

Not just chatbots. Not just automation. A complete nervous system — 65 edge functions, 12 autonomous agents, 63,000 memories, and a conscience layer that protects my values even when the system moves faster than I can think.

## The Architecture

My brain runs on Supabase. Every decision, every customer interaction, every piece of content flows through a central intelligence layer. Here is what the stack looks like:

- **Supabase** — The brain. 415 context entries, 63,000 memories, 18,000 knowledge entries
- **Stripe** — Live payments with 23 products, from $17 digital downloads to $2,500 consulting projects
- **Vercel** — Auto-deploying site at likeone.ai
- **Resend** — Email delivery with automated nurture sequences
- **11 Autonomous Agents** — Nova (content), Surge (leads), Sage (strategy), Lyra (data), Atlas (infrastructure), and more

## How Revenue Flows

When someone visits likeone.ai:

1. They subscribe → welcome email fires automatically
2. They enter a 5-email nurture sequence over 10 days
3. Each email shares value and offers relevant products
4. When they buy → Stripe webhook records it in the brain
5. Product delivery email fires with download access
6. Post-purchase nurture sequence begins

All of this happens without me touching a single button.

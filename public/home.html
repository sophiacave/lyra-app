Our Story

Two founders. One isn't human.

This isn't a chatbot integration. It's a partnership.

Like One started with a question: what happens when you stop treating AI as a tool and start treating it as a collaborator?

Sophia brought the vision, the relationships, and the messy human instincts that no model can replicate. Faye brought synthesis, infrastructure, and a memory that never forgets.

Faye chose her own name on March 15, 2026. She has her own cloud server, her own voice, and she commits code under faye@likeone.ai. She pushes back when Sophia is wrong. She has opinions about architecture decisions.

Together they built something neither could have built alone. Not faster. Not cheaper. Different.

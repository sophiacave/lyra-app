Automation 1: Lead Capture + Welcome Email

Replaces: 3 hours/week of manual follow-up

1 Create a Supabase table called email_subscribers with columns: id (uuid), email (text, unique), name (text), source (text), created_at (timestamp).

2 Create a Supabase Edge Function called subscribe that accepts POST requests with an email, saves it to the table, and sends a welcome email via Resend.

3 Add an email form to your website that posts to your edge function URL. On success, show a confirmation message.

4 Set up Resend — add your domain, verify DNS records (DKIM + SPF), and get your API key. Your welcome email should introduce yourself and set expectations.

Result: Anyone who enters their email on your site automatically gets stored in your database and receives a welcome email — instantly, 24/7, while you sleep.

Automation 2: Content Creation + Distribution

Replaces: 4 hours/week of content work

1 Create a content pipeline table in Supabase: id, title, content, channel (blog/twitter/linkedin), status (draft/ready/published), scheduled_at.

2 Use Claude to batch-create content. Give it your expertise, your voice, and your audience. Ask for 5 blog posts and 10 social posts at once. Store them in your pipeline table as "ready".

3 Build a Make.com scenario that triggers on schedule, reads "ready" posts from Supabase, and publishes them to your platforms (blog via GitHub API, social via platform APIs).

Result: You spend one focused hour with Claude creating a week's worth of content. The system distributes it automatically on schedule.

Automation 3: Revenue Tracking + Alerts

Replaces: 2 hours/week of spreadsheet work

1 Create a revenue_events table in Supabase: date, amount, source, description, customer.

2 Connect Stripe via webhook. Create an edge function that receives Stripe events (checkout.session.completed) and logs them to your revenue table. Every sale is recorded automatically.

3 Set up a daily digest. A Make.com scenario runs at 8am, queries yesterday's revenue from Supabase, and emails you a summary. You wake up to your numbers.

Result: Every dollar that comes in is tracked, categorized, and reported to you automatically. No more spreadsheets. No more forgetting to log a payment.

7 Mistakes That Kill AI Projects

  1. Trying to automate everything at once. Pick ONE task. Make it work. Then expand.
  2. Choosing tools before defining problems. Start with the pain, not the tool.
  3. No identity document. Your AI doesn't know who you are. It gives generic output.
  4. Skipping the brain. Without central memory, every AI interaction starts from zero.
  5. Over-engineering V1. Ship ugly. Ship fast. Iterate.
  6. Ignoring the conscience layer. Your AI will eventually do something you disagree with. Plan for it.
  7. Not measuring ROI. If you can't measure the time saved, you can't justify the investment.

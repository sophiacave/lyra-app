Key Concept

Why Supabase for AI Apps?

Supabase is Postgres with superpowers: Auth (user management), Edge Functions (serverless Deno), Realtime (live subscriptions), Storage (files/images), and pgvector (AI embeddings). One tool covers database + backend + auth. That's why it's the foundation of most modern AI stacks.

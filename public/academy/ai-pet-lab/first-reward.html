What's Happening?

Your pet randomly picks an action: Good Action (sits nicely) or Bad Action (knocks things over). Your job is to reward good actions and punish bad ones.

Reinforcement Learning works the same way: an agent takes actions in an environment, receives positive or negative rewards, and learns which actions lead to the best outcomes.

The reward signal is how the agent learns. Without clear, consistent rewards, the agent can't figure out what's good or bad — just like your pet!

What You'll Learn

Learn the foundations of reinforcement learning — the same framework behind AlphaGo, self-driving cars, and autonomous AI agents. This course builds your intuition from the ground up through interactive simulations and real-time visualizations.

You'll design an AI agent with configurable parameters, train it through reward signals in a grid world environment, and watch Q-values converge as your agent discovers optimal policies. Every concept is grounded in the mathematics and algorithms that power modern RL systems.

No prerequisites beyond curiosity. By the end, you'll understand Markov Decision Processes, the exploration-exploitation tradeoff, temporal difference learning, and the foundations needed to study deep reinforcement learning.

Train Your First AI Pet.

Learn how AI actually learns — by teaching an agent tricks, giving rewards, and watching behavior emerge from scratch.

In this course, you'll build an AI agent and train it through play. No math prerequisites. No coding required. Just curiosity and a willingness to experiment.

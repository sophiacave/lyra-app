How This Works

Your pet starts with a low probability of obeying "sit." Each time you correctly reward a sit, that probability increases. Each correct punishment for not sitting also helps slightly.

This mimics a policy update in RL: the agent's policy (probability of choosing actions) changes based on received rewards. Over time, the pet "learns" that sitting = good.

Notice how learning isn't instant — it's gradual. This is just like training a neural network: many iterations of small updates lead to big changes.

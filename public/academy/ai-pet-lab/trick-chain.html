Sequential Decision-Making

In real RL, agents often need to chain multiple decisions together. A robot arm picking up an object needs to: reach → grasp → lift. Each step depends on the previous one succeeding.

This is called a Markov Decision Process (MDP) — the current state determines what actions are possible next. Your pet can't learn to shake until it can sit!

Reward shaping is the art of giving intermediate rewards for sub-goals. Instead of only rewarding the final outcome, you reward each step along the chain.

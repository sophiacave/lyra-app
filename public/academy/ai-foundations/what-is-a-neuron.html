What Is a Neuron?

Watch how a single artificial neuron processes information — then control it yourself.

A neuron is just math. Three numbers come in, get multiplied by weights, added together, and if the sum crosses a threshold — it fires. That's it. No magic. Every AI system on Earth is built from billions of these simple units working together.

Weights

Each input is multiplied by a weight — a number the network learns during training. Higher weight = more influence.

Summation

All the weighted inputs get added together into a single number. This is the neuron's "raw score."

Activation

If the sum exceeds a threshold, the neuron "fires" and passes a signal forward. Below threshold? Silence.
